## Key Strategy

### Problem-First Approach
**Never sell the solution before the HCP recognizes the problem.**

- Section 1 establishes the patient need (disease plateau)
- Section 2 explains why standard approach fails (1 pathway)
- Section 3 validates why Vabysmo works (2 pathways, 2x duration, 2.3x response)
- Section 4 captures interest (training request)

### Mechanism Advantage
**Education > Sales**

Vabysmo's unique value = **bispecific (2 pathways)** vs monotherapy (1 pathway)

- Animated mechanism comparison (Section 2) shows the difference
- Clinical data (Section 3) proves the advantage
- Training CTA (Section 4) deepens expertise

### Multi-Channel Traffic
**HCPs engage differently across channels**

- **Website banner:** Passive awareness (doctors browsing journals)
- **Email banner:** Active engagement (targeted list outreach)
- **LinkedIn:** Professional network (peer influence)
- **WhatsApp:** Mobile-first messaging (quick engagement)

All drive to same Snak™ experience for consistency.
